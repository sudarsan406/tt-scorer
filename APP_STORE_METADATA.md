## Screenshot Design Guidelines

### Color Scheme for Annotations:
- **Background overlay:** Semi-transparent dark gradient at top/bottom
- **Text color:** White for high contrast
- **Accent color:** #2196F3 (your app's primary blue)

### Font Recommendations:
- **Title text:** San Francisco Display Bold (iOS system font) - 48pt
- **Subtitle text:** San Francisco Display Regular - 32pt

### Layout:
- **Portrait orientation only** (as configured in app.json)
- **Annotations at top or bottom** - don't cover key UI elements
- **Consistent style across all screenshots**

### Tools for Creating Screenshots:
1. **Method 1: Real Device/Simulator**
   - Run app on iPhone 14 Pro Max simulator (6.5" display)
   - Take screenshots using Cmd+S
   - Dimensions will be correct automatically

2. **Method 2: Design Tools**
   - Use Figma, Sketch, or Photoshop
   - Create frames at 1290 x 2796 pixels
   - Add screenshot + annotation overlays

3. **Method 3: App Preview Tools**
   - Use [screenshots.pro](https://screenshots.pro)
   - Use [AppLaunchpad](https://theapplaunchpad.com)
   - Upload screenshots and add text overlays

## Final Tips for App Review Success

1. **Lead with tournament management** - This is your strongest differentiator
2. **Emphasize ELO system** - Explain it's competitiveness-adjusted (unique)
3. **Show professional features** - Charts, exports, analytics
4. **Use professional language** - "Tournament management" not "score tracker"
5. **Highlight offline/privacy** - No account, no tracking, all local
6. **Be specific in review notes** - Help reviewers understand uniqueness

Your app has genuine value - the metadata just needs to communicate that clearly!
